Hi!

This time we, Techno-Torques, received the `FEETECH STS3032` motor for driving from `FEETECH RC Model Co., Ltd.` (hereinafter referred to as `Feetech RC`).

In this article, we will introduce `Feetech RC` and the motor they provided us

# About Feetech RC

![Feetech RC Logo](https://www.feetechrc.com/Data/feetechrc/template/en/images/logo.png)

[Official Website](https://www.feetechrc.com/)

`Feetech RC` is a company based in Shenzhen, China, that develops and manufactures servo motors.
They provide high-quality servo motors for various fields such as robotics, drones, and RC cars.

# About STS3032

The `FEETECH STS3032` we received is a serial servo motor that supports continuous rotation.

Our team used the `FEETECH FB5311M-360` servo motor from the same company in last year's RCJJ competition.
However, this motor was large in size and narrowed the camera's field of view, presenting a challenge.

The new `FEETECH STS3032` is compact yet has high torque, allowing for increased freedom in robot design.
Additionally, it is expected to Timprove the accuracy of line tracing.

# Usage Method

The `FEETECH STS3032` is controlled via serial communication.
The communication method is "Half Duplex Asynchronous Serial Communication".

As feedback, the following information can be obtained:

- Position
- Temperature
- Speed
- Voltage
- Load
