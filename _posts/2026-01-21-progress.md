Hi!

It's been a long time since we last updated our site.

Over the past few months we've made great progress. Here's an update.

# In recent months
## Our first tournament

We prepared for and entered our first competition, the [East Tokyo tournament](https://rcjj-kanto.org/tokyo/).

![Our robot at the East Tokyo tournament](/assets/images/2026-01-21-robot1.jpg)

We placed 10th out of 17 teams with a score of 308 points.

We couldn't perform our best because the Raspberry Pi on our robot shut down during the second run.

Fortunately, because it shut down just before we were about to start, we were able to restart it and resume the run from the middle.

## Our second tournament

Despite the troubles at the East Tokyo tournament, we were able to compete in the [Kanto tournament](https://rcjj-kanto.org/).

[Our team's poster submitted to the Kanto tournament(Japanese)](/assets/2026-kanto-poster.pdf)

Despite unexpected issues, we placed 1st out of 14 teams with a score of 408 points.

This was a great achievement for us, and we're very happy.

We also received the "Robocuper Award", which is given to the team with the best poster.

# Finally
Thanks for reading!

We'll keep improving our robot and will post updates in the future.
